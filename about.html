Help Disabled

Every person feels the desire to be in an inclusive space surrounded by supportive people and when it comes to special needs, education and medical help go hand-in-hand. TSF is a non-profitable organization that works for the welfare of specially-abled (differently-abled). We provide them education as well as therapy to students who are struggling with different kinds of needs. We also provide vocational training in trades like pottery, tailoring, etc. After completion of the course, the trainee are offered jobs in external or self-employement. Thus help them attain economic independence.
